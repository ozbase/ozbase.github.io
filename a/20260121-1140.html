Micron to Acquire Powerchip’s Taiwanese Fab for $1.8B

Source: https://pc.watch.impress.co.jp/docs/news/2079073.html

AI Analysis

Micron Technology has signed a letter of intent to acquire the P5 fabrication site from Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation (PSMC) in Tongluo, Taiwan, for $1.8 billion in cash. The acquisition includes a 300 mm fab cleanroom (~300,000 sq ft) and is intended to boost DRAM production capacity as global demand continues to outpace supply. Micron expects the deal to close by Q2 2026, pending regulatory approvals, with meaningful DRAM wafer output starting in the second half of 2027. The agreement also establishes a long-term collaboration between Micron and PSMC covering assembly and legacy DRAM support. The Tongluo facility’s proximity to Micron’s existing Taiwan operations may enable operational synergies.
